Our Story

Where Every Cup
Tells a Story

Born from a love of good coffee and great conversations, Kedai Kopi began as a small corner shop in 2018. Today, we continue to hand-roast every batch, source directly from local farmers, and pour love into every cup we serve.

  • Single-origin beans, ethically sourced
  • Small-batch roasting for maximum freshness
  • Baristas trained in specialty coffee artistry
  • Cozy atmosphere designed for comfort
